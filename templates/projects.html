OMC Autos

OLAWORE MOTOR COMPANY (OMC) is a Nigerian auto manufacturing start-up founded in 2018 with the aim of designing and producing affordable and durable vehicles for the African market. As the first Nigerian brand to venture into the realm of sports and supercars, OMC is a true game - changer in the Nigerian automotive industry bringing a refreshing wave of customization, in-house innovation and alternative energy vehicles.
